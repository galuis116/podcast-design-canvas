Playback Consequences

Quiet reminder, not an export block A marked pickup with no take stays visible as a reminder, but it does not become a generic blocked-export state.
Caption handoff stays scoped Only the replaced span reopens caption review when the new words need confirmation.
Never swap silently The creator always confirms the seam before the original line is replaced in the finished episode.

Connected workflow

  • speaker bucket stays tied to ingest and role mapping
  • look and framing stay governed by matching and framing specs
  • caption refresh stays limited to the replaced words
  • accepted pickups show up as meaningful version-history checkpoints
